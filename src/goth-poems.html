The Darkness

The darkness consumes me,

A never-ending void of black.

It wraps around my soul,

And holds me tight in its grasp.

Solitude

In the silence of the night,

I am alone with my thoughts.

Here, in the solitude of the darkness,

I am free to be myself.

The Shadows

In the shadows, I hide away,

A creature of the night.

Here, in the darkness, I am free,

To be who I truly am.

The Night

In the stillness of the night,

I feel the darkness calling to me.

I am drawn to its embraces,

And I am at peace.

The Moon

Under the light of the moon,

I am at one with the night.

Its eerie glow washes over me,

And I am at home.

The Darkness Within

Deep within the depths of my soul,

Lies a darkness that I cannot escape.

It consumes me, it controls me,

But I cannot help but embrace it.

The Pretender

I put on a smile and a mask,

Pretending to be happy and carefree.

But deep inside, I am broken,

And no one knows the truth.

The Erased Epitaph

I was once here, I once existed,

But now my name has been erased.

My tombstone stands forgotten,

A forgotten relic of the past.

The Stone Age Mason

In the days of old, I was a mason,

Crafting stone into works of art.

But now, I am a forgotten memory,

Lost to the ravages of time.

Stone Age Day Dreams

In my daydreams, I am back in the past,

In the days of the stone age.

I am a hunter, a gatherer,

Living in harmony with nature.

Tripping Again

I close my eyes and let go,

Drifting off into a dreamlike state.

I am free, I am weightless,

Trapped in a world of my own making.

The Eternal Night

In the depths of the night,

I am lost in an endless void.

Here, in the embrace of the darkness,

I am free from the constraints of the world.

The Gothic Heart

My heart beats with the rhythm of the night,

A gothic symphony of darkness and despair.

I am a creature of the shadows,

Forever lost in the embrace of the night.

The Silent Darkness

In the silence of the night,

I am consumed by the darkness.

It wraps around me like a cloak,

Muffling the sounds of the world.
